About Expressive Encounters Workshop 2024

This Workshop aims to advance the development of real-world applications involving virtual and embodied agents by focusing on generating socially appropriate non-verbal behavior, considering cultural and personal factors, and emphasizing real-time processing for user acceptance and trust.

TRACK SCOPES
Our tracks provide a comprehensive exploration of behavior generation for virtual and embodied agents. These tracks focus on developing techniques for real-time non-verbal behavior generation, addressing personal factors such as culture and personality, and deploying gesture generation on real-world embodied agents. Each track offers researchers a platform to collaborate and innovate, bridging disciplines like computer vision, natural language processing, and human-computer interaction to create more socially aware and culturally sensitive agent interactions.

TOPICS
The main suggested topics for the workshop include, but are not limited to:

  • Vision, audio, and text-based data processing for gesture generation
  • Vision, audio, and text-based data modelling for culture detection and understanding personality
  • Real-time gesture generation techniques
  • New generative models to model personal factors (e.g., culture, personality)
  • Incorporating culture and personality in generated gestures
  • Gesture generation in small groups
  • Techniques to transfer human-human interactions to human-agent interactions
  • Transfer learning and transformer techniques to apply virtual agents' gestures to reduced degrees of freedom embodied agents
  • Vision, audio, and text-based data modelling techniques for detecting trust and openness of humans towards embodied agents
